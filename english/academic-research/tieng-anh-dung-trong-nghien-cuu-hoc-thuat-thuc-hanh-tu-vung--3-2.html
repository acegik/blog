Tiếng Anh dùng trong nghiên cứu học thuật - Thực hành từ vựng 3.2

by pnhung177 — on English for Academic Research , Nouns — 01/10/2014

Bài tập thực hành này tiếp tục về danh từ (noun), với những từ sau: basis, degree, extent, level, region. Mục đích của bài này nhằm giúp người đọc phân biệt các trường hợp sử dụng giữa degree vs. level, level vs. basis vs. extent, basis vs. region.

Nội dung bài thực hành

  1. At the conceptual basis / level / degree it is certainly true that females show greater aptitude to learning languages than males.
  2. New ways of working from home are attracting growing interest on an international basis / level / region.
  3. We investigated how the severity of memory impairment was affected by variations in the level / degree / extent of damage to the medial temporal lobe memory system.
  4. To a large degree / level / extent, the choice of interaction style should relate to the user requirements.
  5. Poverty levels used to be of concern, but in recent years goverment have, to a large basis / extent / region, neglected the problem.
  6. MR imaging is sensitive to alternations in the motion of water molecules in the extent / region / level of the brain studied.
  7. We found that pollution values in the region / extent / level of Prydz Bay, Antarctica have increased by 22% in the last two years.
  8. All related links will be posted on the course website on the regular extent / basis / level.
  9. Reviews should be carried out both at the region / level / basis of the entire program and at the degree / extent / level of the individual researchers.
  10. We investigate to what degree / extent / level being bilingual means being more tolerant and open to the world.

Một vài chú thích

Nghĩa của một số từ:

  • Extent: the space or degree to which a thing extends; quy mô, phạm vi.
  • Molecule (/´mɔli¸kju:l/): two or more atoms held together by chemical bonds; phân tử.
  • Impairment: The result of being impaired; become worse; sự suy yếu, sự sút kém.
  • Poverty (/ˈpɒvəti/): condition of being poor; the state or condition of having little or no money, goods, or means of support; (cảnh bần hàn).
  • Neglect: to pay no attention or too little attention to; bỏ bê, bỏ mặc.
  • MR imaging: Magnetic Resonance Imaging; chụp ảnh cộng hưởng từ.
  • Sensitive: (photographic paper is highly sensitive to light).
  • Bilingual (/bai´liηgwəl/): Having the ability to speak two languages; có khả năng nói hai thứ tiếng.
